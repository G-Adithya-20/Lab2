Sharan Nair, Shyamolie Parikh, Fahim Rasheed, Aryan Nair

                                      4 friends who had 1 meaningful conversation and decided to start “this is not a podcast”. We talk about our personal lives, events taking place across the world and basically provide our unsolicited opinion on anything and everything. If you are lonely, want to fill in the silences, kill time, learn about the most random things, this podcast is for you.

Hosted by Sharan Nair and three friends who once had a surprisingly deep conversation—and, in a moment of overconfidence and chaos, decided the world needed to hear more of it. That one chat spiraled into This Is Not A Podcast: a space where four fairly average people talk about absolutely anything, everything, and occasionally... nothing at all. We’re not journalists, philosophers, or life coaches—we’re just four friends navigating adulthood, culture, the internet, and our own lives one tangent at a time. Expect raw conversations, wild detours, questionable takes, personal confessions, and the kind of banter that feels like you're right there with us on the group call. Some episodes are funny. Some are surprisingly deep. Some might leave you wondering why you're still listening—but hey, isn’t that what the best group chats feel like? So if you:

  • need a break from the serious stuff,
  • want to feel like you're in a room with friends,
  • love hearing different perspectives (or just random trivia no one asked for),
  • or simply want to fill the silence while doing dishes or pretending to work...

Then congrats—you’ve found your people. This might not be a podcast in the traditional sense, but it’s definitely something.


Host profile section

His videos with his grandmother, especially the "Grandma Reacts" series with Netflix, brought him into the spotlight. His creative and humorous style has led to collaborations with brands like Pizza Hut and Netflix India.
